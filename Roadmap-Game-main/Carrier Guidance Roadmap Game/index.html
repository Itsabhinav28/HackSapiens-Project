School

  • Take Physics, Chemistry, and Maths
  • Participate in science fairs and competitions
  • Engage in coding and engineering projects
  • Seek internships or part-time work in related fields
  • Maintain a strong academic record

JEE Preparation

  • Study the JEE syllabus thoroughly
  • Take regular practice tests
  • Join a coaching class or study group
  • Review and revise key concepts frequently
  • Stay healthy and manage stress effectively

BTech College & Degree

  • Focus on core engineering subjects
  • Participate in labs and practical sessions
  • Work on group projects and presentations
  • Attend workshops and seminars
  • Build a network with professors and peers

Job Hunting & Companies

  • Prepare a professional resume and cover letter
  • Apply for internships and entry-level positions
  • Attend career fairs and company presentations
  • Practice interview skills and techniques
  • Build a strong LinkedIn profile and network

Becoming a Successful Engineer

  • Continuously update your skills and knowledge
  • Seek mentorship and guidance from experienced engineers
  • Work on real-world projects and problems
  • Stay curious and embrace lifelong learning
  • Contribute to and lead engineering teams effectively
